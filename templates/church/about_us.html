A BRIEF ABOUT US


The Christ Resurrection Chapel, FMCK held its first service 20 years ago on the (insert date) ………………, with 5 people in attendance, on an Easter Sunday (Resurrection Sunday). From humble beginnings, the TCRC has grown over the years, and the Church stands firm by the grace of the Almighty God. Initially known as the Christian Chapel, it was subsequently aptly named “The Christ Resurrection Chapel, FMCK” (TCRC) and incorporated in 2003 (a symbolic recognition of her beginnings)


The TCRC is a place of discovery, recovery, and fulfilment in the kingdom of God. The Church is fulfilling Christ’s mandate of reaching the lost, making disciples of men, and equipping the body of Christ. he TCRC regularly conducts medical outreaches to communities within and outside Nasarawa state, aside in-reaches within the hospital.


The TCRC is a community-oriented, loving, and compassionate church. We place Christ first and depend on the Holy Spirit for empowerment and upliftment. At the TCRC, we give attention to prayer and ministry of the word. (Acts 6: 50). We are committed to love the Lord, the brethren, and our neighbors. (Matt 22:37-39, 1 Peter 1: 22). We praise God for his presence and impartation at our weekly meetings and special activities. Our weekly meetings and calendar of activities is available for viewing on our website.


We welcome you to worship with us, it would be a life-changing experience. You can also access our sermons and special programs via the links provided below.
